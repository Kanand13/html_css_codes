Our Popular Choices

Espresso

A concentrated coffee brewed by forcing hot water through finely-ground coffee beans. It's strong & rich in flavor,served in small shots.A hallmark of well-prepared espresso is the crema, a creamy layer that forms on top of the shot, adding to its rich texture. Typically served in small 1-ounce shots, espresso boasts an intense flavor profile with notes of caramel, chocolate, nuts, and fruit. Despite its robust taste, espresso contains less caffeine than drip coffee but still provides a significant energy boost.

Americano

Made by diluting espresso with hot water, resulting in a coffee with a similar strength to drip coffee but with a different flavor profile.This dilutes the espresso and creates a coffee beverage with a flavor profile similar to drip coffee but with the intensity of espresso. The ratio of espresso to water can be adjusted based on personal preference. Some enjoy it with just a single shot of espresso, while others prefer a double shot for a stronger flavor. Overall, the Americano is a simple yet satisfying coffee drink that can be customized to suit individual tastes.

Cappuccino

A classic Italian coffee drink consisting of equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam. It has a creamy texture and a strong coffee flavor.Brewed with precision, it offers a harmonious blend of rich espresso and creamy milk, topped with airy foam. Traditionally served in smaller cups, the cappuccino provides a comforting and indulgent experience, making it a favorite choice for coffee lovers around the world.

Mocha

A chocolate-flavored variant of a latte, made with espresso, steamed milk, and chocolate syrup. It's often topped with whipped cream.Made by adding chocolate syrup or cocoa powder to a shot of espresso, then topped with steamed milk and optionally whipped cream, the mocha offers a perfect balance of sweetness and bitterness. Whether enjoyed as a morning treat or an afternoon pick-me-up, the mocha provides a satisfying and decadent coffee experience for aficionados seeking a touch of indulgence.
